About Finess

Founded in 2024 by Jack Daniels, Finess aims to redefine fashion by bringing the best of vintage streetwear to modern wardrobes. Our dedicated team, including George, Alice, and Jessica, is passionate about preserving the authenticity and exclusivity of timeless fashion.

At Finess, we believe that fashion should be a form of self-expression, and our goal is to provide our customers with the most unique and sought-after vintage pieces, from limited-edition sneakers to classic graphic tees and one-of-a-kind accessories.
